ABOUT PORSCHE

Porsche is the most successful manufacturer of exclusive sports cars and has an
outstanding reputation. This success is based on the desirability of the vehicles,
but equally importantly, due to its unique corporate culture that is shaped by
integrity and compliance. And it should stay that way. All board members,
members of the management bodies, managers and employees make their
own contributions every day according to the motto:
Sporty and fair - that's typical Porsche.
